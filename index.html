About

Over the years, I’ve worn many hats—developer, entrepreneur, creator, and dreamer.

My journey began with an unshakable curiosity about how technology could solve real-world problems and enhance creativity.

As a professional React Native programmer, I’ve had the privilege of building intuitive, scalable apps that provide real value to users. Whether it’s creating seamless mobile experiences with Expo and Supabase or experimenting with innovative app ideas like AI-powered music players and interactive games, I’ve always sought to push the boundaries of what’s possible.

But it’s not just about code. It’s about solving meaningful problems.

From brainstorming a platform that replaces traditional exam dockets with digital solutions to designing a chatbot-powered money remittance service for cross-border transactions, I’ve embraced the challenge of blending technology with human needs.

One of my favorite projects was developing a trap beat themed around life in the 2020s—it taught me the power of blending technical precision with raw creativity.

Whether I’m working on a drone design for photography enthusiasts, a rhythm-based game like Rhythm Tiles, or a new app like Witterverse, my goal remains the same: to create something that resonates.

As I continue this journey, I’m eager to collaborate with like-minded innovators and turn more ideas into reality.

If you’re passionate about tech, creativity, or simply building something meaningful, let’s connect—I’d love to hear your story too.
